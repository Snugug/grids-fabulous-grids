--- footnote: > [Sven Wolfermann, Grids & Glory](http://maddesigns.de/css-grids/#/) notes: > * Float * No direct layout control * Pushes items either towards the left or the right for indirect layout control * No built-in concept of spacing * Flexbox * Lays out items in one dimension * Size and position based on content size * Cannot control positioning in more that one direction * No built-in concept of spacing * Grids * Lays out items in two dimensions * Set constraints for size and position * Can directly control positioning in each direction * Has a built-in concept of spacing ---
